We use only the most ethically sourced beans.

All of our baristas receive world class training, competitive pay, and flexible schedules.

We encourage the development of community through our cozy atmosphere, concerts, events, art shows, and more.

As you might know by the name, we are a Christian non-profit with proceeds going to directly to the City of Children in Ensenada, Mexico.

"Some friends and I decided to create Boanerges Coffee House after discussing what we could do to help the City of Children in Ensenada, Mexico. It was a place we felt so passionately about, as well as coffee, which led to this. Now, we hope to use this space for ministry, good coffee, and good works."
- Grant Stewart, Founder and CEO
